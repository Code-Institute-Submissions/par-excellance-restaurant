Par Excellance

By Valeria Bonié

London’s First Alergen-free Fine Dining Restaurant

Welcome to ParExcellance - Europe's leading alergen-free fine dining restaurant in London! Here you can safely enjoy some of the finest recipes of European cuisine. All our dishes are house-made and we source only organic, high-quality ingredients. Eat clean, stay elegant and enjoy your health!

Menu Created by Our In-house Team of Chefs

Every dish in our restaurant is gluten-free, dairy-free, egg-free, nut-free and soy-free. Instead of using refined sugar and syrups, we stick to natural sweeteners such as dried fruits and honey. Our in-house team of chefs lead by Chef de Cuisine Valeria Bonié has more than 15 years of experience in preparing fine-dining delicacies. Feel free to let us know about your other intolerances so we can adjust the menu just for you.
